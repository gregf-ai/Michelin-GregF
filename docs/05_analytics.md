# Analytics

## 1. Financial Analytics

### Core Questions
- Does Michelin structurally outperform on margin quality?
- Is Michelin an efficient allocator of capital (ROIC)?
- Is value being returned to shareholders over time?
- Are top-line and profit growth consistent versus peers?

### Metrics in App
- EBITDA Margin
- Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)
- Dividend Growth
- Annual Stock Growth
- Revenue Growth
- EBITDA Growth

### Comparison Logic
- Primary peer ranking uses 10-year median.
- Optional 2025 basis toggle for point-in-time comparison.
- Trend panel defaults to Michelin + top 2 peers.
- Selected bar drills into company-specific historical view.
